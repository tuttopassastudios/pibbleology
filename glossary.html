Glossary

For broader terms, see also the Terminology section of the main article.

The following terms are used throughout Pibbleology. Definitions reflect community usage as of the most recent contribution that nobody has yet objected to.

A

Abstain
Pibble's preferred political position in any vote, including votes not currently being held. By convention, an abstention by Pibble is recorded as present.
Always-Was Doctrine
The majority position on Pibble's origin, holding that Pibble predates the archive and any attempt to archive Pibble. Opposed by the Recent Pibble position.
Adjacent (canon)
Tier II canon. Material accepted by long-running contributors without core-source backing. See Canon disputes.

C

Canon-but-tired
An informal canon tier reserved for material that is universally accepted and that nobody wishes to discuss further.
Caller, The
A voice on a telephone that has not rung. See Minor figures.
Cycle, Spoon
The sequence of fragments concerning the Second Spoon. The narrative arc of the Middle Era.

F

First Rule, The
If Pibble is in the room, the room is now about Pibble. Descriptive, not prescriptive.
Footnote-only entities
Figures who appear in the archive solely through footnotes; sometimes promoted, never quite demoted.

I

Index Boy
The figure who quietly re-alphabetizes anything left unattended. See Minor figures.
Indexer
Any contributor who insists on order. Treated with cautious gratitude; not formally appointed.

N

Not a Pibble
The community designation for any cryptid, figure, or rumor that is sometimes mistaken for Pibble and is not. Maintained for clarity; no entries are currently disputed.

P

Pibblegram
A short written message that arrives one day before it was sent. Replies, if any, must be sent one day before they are written.
Pibblesphere
The notional region within which Pibble is the dominant topic of conversation. Expands to fit any room Pibble enters.
Pibblet question
The longest open dispute in Pibbleology. Concerns whether Pibblet exists, and if so, when.
Porch (Pre-Index)
A porch sat upon by Pibble three weeks before its construction. See Porch Incident.

R

Reading Hour
A recurring period during which nothing reads anything. Duration approximate.
Recent Pibble
The minority dating of Pibble to a specific, mundane afternoon. Opposed by the Always-Was Doctrine.

S

Scroll Zero
The earliest cited fragment of the archive. See Lore Timeline.
Speculative (canon)
Tier III canon. Material introduced by one contributor and humored by the rest.
Soup, Unordered
The soup whose temperature Pibble complains of in Scroll Zero, fragment 3a; never ordered by anyone, including Pibble.
