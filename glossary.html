Pibble Glossary

Definitions for the words and accounts that recur in Pibble memes.

Terms

Pibble
Internet slang for a French bulldog puppy, often pictured lying on its back with its belly up. Originally Doggo-Speak for pitbull, the meaning shifted in 2023 after the Handheld Pupper photo went viral.
Doggo-Speak
A late-2010s internet trend of cute, baby-talk-style nicknames for dog breeds and behaviors (e.g., doggo, floofer, pupper, pibble).
Gmail
A nonsensical caption used for white Pibble images (often white with black spots). Popularized by TikTok creator @jpibblemafia's August 2024 Gmail, you naughty pibble, come back Soft Irony video.
Handheld Pupper
A 2022–2023 viral photograph of a minuscule French bulldog puppy held in one hand. Started the use of Pibble for French bulldog puppies. Also called Geeble.
Geeble
An alternate name for Handheld Pupper, used especially in Alienposting / Zeep Glorp edits where the puppy is colored green and given antennas.
T-Pose Puppy
Also STOP FIGHTING!! — an image macro series featuring a cross-eyed French bulldog puppy in a T-pose. Began circulating May 2024; went viral on X in July 2024.
I Am Pibble, Wash My Belly
A viral TikTok audio from April 2025 of a baby voice saying I am Pibble, wash my belly. Spawned remix variants such as Wash My Belly, Yay and Clean My Belly.
Pibble Death / Pibble is Dead
An October 2025 internet rumor that Pibble had died. The dog in the original Handheld Pupper photo is alive, per @pibble12 and the dog's owner.
Papyrus Irony
A meme format pairing serious or absurd text with the Papyrus font, used in the fake Gmails and Gmail, you naughty pibble Pibble memes.
Soft Irony
A meme aesthetic that pairs cute or wholesome imagery with deliberately mismatched text, of which the Gmail, you naughty pibble video is an example.
Alienposting / Zeep Glorp
A meme format in which a subject (often Handheld Pupper) is crudely edited green with antennas and treated as an alien — the origin of the Geeble name.

Accounts

@pibble12 (Instagram)
The Instagram account whose August 10, 2023 first post — the Handheld Pupper image — popularized Pibble as a name for French bulldog puppies. Often credited as the creator of the modern Pibble meme.
@jmarcoz (Instagram)
A French bulldog breeder whose videos source many of the Pibble Reels reposted by @pibble12.
@813_frenchies_ (Instagram)
The Instagram account that first posted the Handheld Pupper photo, on October 17, 2022.
@jpibblemafia (TikTok)
TikToker behind the August 12, 2024 Gmail, you naughty pibble, come back Soft Irony video. ~252,600 likes.
@leakyscrotum (TikTok)
TikToker behind the June 25, 2025 What Kind of X Is This? Pibble video. 220,500+ likes.
@hero_the_pibble (X)
The earliest known X account to use the word Pibble for a pitbull, on August 3, 2008.
@SirPatStew (X)
Actor Patrick Stewart's X account, which on March 9, 2017 posted the first viral tweet using the term pibble (for his foster pitbull Ginger).
