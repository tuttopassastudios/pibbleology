Glossary of the Pibble Verse

For the central concept, see Pibble. For full species profiles, see Variants & Species.

The following terms are used across the Pibble Verse: on the Pibble Verse Wiki, in TikTok lore videos, and in recurring Reddit threads. Definitions reflect community usage as paraphrased from publicly accessible sources; canonical status is noted where applicable.

B

Bagel
Baby golden retriever within the Pibble Verse taxonomy; a "baby golden pibble" representing a different dog lineage that still fits the small, goofy aesthetic. See Variants § Bagel.
Base Pibble
The "original" brown or tan baby French bulldog. The default referent of pibble when no modifier is given. See Variants § Base Pibble.
Beeble
A bee-adjacent handheld-pibble variant that never stings humans owing to a strong bond with them. Page on the wiki is partially accessible.

C

Canon
Material tagged as official on the Pibble Verse Wiki, written or approved by lorewriters. Currently includes the Pibble and Geeble pages. Should not be edited without permission.
Chis
Hostile entities targeted by geebles; also implicated in the manufacture or naming of Chis Pibble Treats, a poison variant of pibble treats.
Chingles
Hostile entities blasted by geebles using laser guns; paired with Chis in the wiki's Geeble article.
Chis Pibble Treats
A poison version of Pibble Treats, priced at 20 Pibuckies (≈ $40). See Food & Items § Chis Pibble Treats.

E

Emails
An entity listed alongside Chis and Chingles in geeble lore; accessible details are minimal.

F

Fanon
Community interpretations of pibble lore that are not canon-tagged on the wiki. Covers life stages (pibble → pebble), color-based classifications, and additional variants like Oreos / Yoshis. Often originates on TikTok and Reddit.
French bulldog
The base referent of "pibble" — specifically a baby French bulldog — though the meme has expanded to include any small, rotund, silly-looking dog.

G

Geeble
Canon green, antennaed alien creature from Geeblorg; weak powers, can fly, blasts hostile entities with laser guns, likes Pibberries. Acidic blood; very hard, resistant skin.
Geeblius
A language noted on the Pibble Verse Wiki's Pibble page; in Geeblius, "Pibble" is rendered Pibble.
Geeblorg
The alien world from which geebles originate; one of the two primary worlds of the verse, paired with Pibearth.
Gmail
White pibble, often with black spots — among the most recognizable variants in the fandom. Distinct from Oreo / Yoshi, which is predominantly black with white spots.

H

Handheld Pibble
A sub-category of exceptionally small pibbles designed to be carried by humans; the comparison point for the friendly Beeble.

I

"I am Pibble"
One of the two signature catchphrases of pibbles. Used as a self-announcement; treated almost as a verbal compulsion. Forms part of the meme identity and drives much of the community's humor, fanart, and roleplay.

O

Oreo / Yoshi
Black-and-white patterned dog in the pibble taxonomy — predominantly black with white spots. Inverse of the Gmail color pattern.
OreoGlaggle
One of the contributors credited by the Pibble Verse Wiki as a creator / lorewriter.

P

PB Cup
A brand-style character name surfacing in TikTok lore explanations alongside Gmail and Bagel.
Pebble
Older, larger, more mature version of a pibble — a life-stage progression rather than a separate species. Compare the named character "Pebble Mug" introduced in an Instagram reel.
Pibberries
Favorite food of geebles; one of the named foods of the verse.
Pibble
Baby French bulldog (or adjacent small, rotund, silly-looking dog). The central figure of the verse. Canon-tagged on the wiki. See main article.
Pibble Entertainment Headquarters
Companion site / project referenced at pibble.fun.
Pibble Petz
Indie game (itch.io) in which the player finds a stranded pibble, feeds him, washes his belly, and dresses him up to find a new home.
Pibble Treats
The base consumable of the verse, coveted by pibbles and geebles. See Food & Items § Pibble Treats.
Pibble University
Setting from TikTok lore explanations featuring specific characters like "Sir Cha[…]." A local-legend space within the broader verse.
Pibble Verse
The collaborative meme universe centered on pibbles, governed loosely by the Pibble Verse Wiki and expanded by TikTok, Reddit, YouTube, Roblox, and indie games.
Pibearth
Essentially Earth viewed through pibble-centric lore; the default home of pibbles and humans; one of the verse's two primary worlds.
Pibuckies
The Pibble Verse's fictional currency; sample price: 20 Pibuckies (≈ $40) for one Chis Pibble Treat. Origin remains an open question.
Pitbull
A separate real-world dog breed, distinct from pibbles. Pibble lore explicitly rejects the undeserved reputation of pitbulls as vicious.

W

"Wash my belly"
One of the two signature catchphrases of pibbles. A demand for affection, grooming, or pampering; central to the gameplay of Pibble Petz and to most pibble-fan content generally.
Washington
Black pibble, usually solid-colored; the black counterpart to the white Gmail.
