The Founding of Pibbleology

From the boundless creative commons of internet meme culture to a codified worldwide fellowship — the story of how the Verse was set down in writing.

In the Beginning

A Movement Long Foretold

The Pibbleology did not arrive by decree. It emerged — quietly, then all at once — from a culture that had spent decades adoring the small, the squat, and the silly without yet possessing the word for what it was doing.

Short videos, edits, and reposts of French bulldog puppies gave way to something stranger and more durable: a sentient, self-identifying being who announces, simply, I am Pibble. The faith took shape around that single declaration — and around the inevitable request that follows it.

A Brief Chronology

Key Moments in the Founding

Era I · Pre-Canonical

Meme Origins

Internet meme culture surfaces an enduring affection for cute, squat dogs — French bulldogs above all. The Pibble emerges in short-form video and Reddit threads as a sentient being who announces itself and demands its belly washed.

Era II · Distinction

Pibble Is Not Pitbull

Lore explicitly rejects the undeserved stigma facing real-world pitbulls. Pibbles are positioned as a playful, wholesome counter-image to breed prejudice — an early ethical commitment of the faith.

Era III · Codification

The Pibble Verse Wiki

The Lorewriters establish the Pibble Verse Wiki — the canonical archive of the faith. Pages are tagged for canonicity. Rules against griefing, bigotry, and harassment are enshrined.

Era IV · Settlement

The Two Worlds Named

Pibearth and Geeblorg are formally named — the primary cosmological pair of the Verse. Slice-of-life Pibble content takes place on Pibearth; alien cousins (Geebles) inhabit Geeblorg.

Era V · Expansion

The Movement Goes Cross-Media

Pibble Petz launches on itch.io. Roblox accounts propagate. Infinite Craft recipes circulate. TikTok and Instagram reels reach audiences far larger than the wiki itself.

Era VI · Now

A Living Faith

Canon and fanon negotiate in real time. New variants surface and settle. The fellowship continues to grow, on Pibearth and beyond.

The Founders

The Lorewriters

Two names appear above all others in the founding records: OreoGlaggle and DOGGOREVOLUTION. To these Lorewriters we owe the canonical entries that anchor the Verse — Pibble, Geeble, Pibearth, and Geeblorg — and the editorial principles that govern canonical expansion.

The Lorewriters maintain authority over canon-tagged pages. Stewards across the fellowship maintain fanon — the lived, evolving interpretation that fills in the gaps the Lorewriters have not yet sealed.

Cosmology

The Two Worlds of the Verse

Together, Pibearth and Geeblorg form the cosmological dyad upon which the Pibbleology is founded.

Primary World

Pibearth

Essentially Earth, viewed through Pibble-centric lore. The default home of Pibbles and humans, and the implied setting of most slice-of-life Pibble content.

From household carpets to college campuses (notably the Pibble report at UNC Chapel Hill), Pibearth is where the daily belly-wash takes place.

Cousin World

Geeblorg

The alien world associated with our beloved Geebles — green, antennaed, and forever in pursuit of Pibble Treats.

Geeblorg pairs with Pibearth to form the primary cosmological dyad of the faith. Centers on Geeblorg specialize in Pibberry liturgy and cross-species fellowship programs.

Doctrinal Authority

Canon & Fanon

Every faith must distinguish between what has been formally affirmed and what is lived in practice. Pibbleology is no exception.

I.

Canon

Material tagged as canonical on the Pibble Verse Wiki, written or approved by Lorewriters. Currently includes the Pibble and Geeble entries. Not to be edited without Lorewriter permission.

II.

Fanon

Community interpretation that fills in gaps — life stages, color-based classifications, additional variants like Oreos and Yoshis. Often originates on TikTok and Reddit; widely respected within the fellowship.

III.

Open Revelation

Many entries remain stubs — Beebles, Handheld Pibbles, Chis, Chingles, Emails. The Pibuckies origin and the full taxonomy of food and drink remain open questions, awaiting fuller revelation.

Reach

The Faith Across Media

The Pibble walks among us across platforms, games, and worlds.

PRIMARY

The Pibble Verse Wiki

The canonical archive on Fandom, stewarded by the Lorewriters. The doctrinal home of the faith.

SACRAMENTAL GAME

Pibble Petz

An indie title (itch.io) by Kai, damien, SkaldWorks, DeityClown, and PixieCap. The player finds a stranded Pibble, feeds him, washes his belly, and dresses him for a new home.

AVATAR

Roblox

Videos such as MAKING PIBBLE WASH MY BELLY a ROBLOX ACCOUNT embody the Pibble for over a hundred million practitioners.

RECIPE

Infinite Craft

YouTube guides document the combination recipes by which Pibble may be summoned in the Infinite Craft sandbox.

DAILY PRACTICE

TikTok & Instagram

Short-form video drives the ongoing lore growth of the faith — Pibble University, the Pibble report at UNC, and countless daily devotionals.

CROSSOVER

Adjacent Universes

The Pibble has surfaced in My Singing Monsters debates, in series like Ninah, and even in r/ServerBlight — though these crossovers are not canonical.
