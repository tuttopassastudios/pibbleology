A Timeline of Pibble

From a 2008 Doggo-Speak tweet for pitbull to a 2025 death-rumor cycle on TikTok — sixteen years of one very flexible word.

Chronology

Key Dates

  1. August 3, 2008

    First known use on Twitter

    The X account @hero_the_pibble ("Hero the Pibble") posts the earliest known use of the word, meaning pitbull.

  2. February 28, 2014

    Facebook adoption

    The Facebook account Marvel the Super Hero Pibble uses the word as Doggo-Speak for pitbull.

  3. March 14, 2014

    First KYM-tagged photo

    User kkduer uploads the earliest photo tagged pibble in the Know Your Meme database.

  4. March 9, 2017

    Patrick Stewart goes viral

    @SirPatStew tweets a video of his foster pibble Ginger, the first viral tweet to use the term. 14,000+ likes over eight years.

  5. October 17, 2022

    Handheld Pupper posted

    Instagram user @813_frenchies_ posts the Reel that would become the Handheld Pupper / Geeble photo — the seed of the 2023 rebrand.

  6. August 10, 2023

    Pibble means "French bulldog puppy"

    Instagram's @pibble12 shares its first post (the Handheld Pupper) and, later that day, a pibble starting roster slideshow that cements the new meaning.

  7. June 2, 2024

    "Fake Gmails" Reel breaks out

    @pibble12's Pibble eating fake Gmails Reel (sourced from breeder @jmarcoz) earns 56,200+ likes — the account's first notable engagement.

  8. May 2024 — July 2024

    T-Pose Puppy emerges

    The T-Pose Puppy / STOP FIGHTING!! image macro begins circulating in May and explodes on Twitter / X in July.

  9. August 12, 2024

    "Gmail, you naughty pibble"

    TikToker @jpibblemafia posts the Papyrus-font Soft Irony meme that drives Gmail into the lexicon. ~252,600 likes.

  10. April 2025

    "I Am Pibble, Wash My Belly"

    The viral TikTok audio is created. Remixes — Wash My Belly, Yay, Clean My Belly — follow in the months after.

  11. June 25, 2025

    "What kind of pibble is this?"

    TikToker @leakyscrotum posts a niche-trend send-off using the What Kind of X Is This? AI-voice format. 220,500+ likes.

  12. October 2025

    Pibble Death Rumor

    A rumor that Pibble had died spreads on Instagram Reels and TikTok. @pibble12 confirms the original dog is alive.
