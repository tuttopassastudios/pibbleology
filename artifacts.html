Food, Drinks & Items

For the species that consume these items, see Variants & Species. For currency context, see § Pibuckies.

The Pibble Verse has a small but consequential economy: a fictional currency called Pibuckies, a base consumable called Pibble Treats, and at least one poisoned counterpart sold by hostile parties. The combination of tasty treats and poison variants fits with the playful — but occasionally dangerous — tone of the universe.[13][10]

The Pibble Verse Wiki's Food and Drinks page is referenced but only partially accessible; the entries below combine the surfaced excerpts with cross-references from the Geeble article and community posts.

Pibble Treats

Pibble Treats are the base consumable of the verse, functioning as a core resource that characters crave and compete over. They appear throughout pibble and geeble lore: geebles in particular hover around skyscrapers asking humans for pibble treats, and simply fly away if refused.[10][13]

Chis Pibble Treats (poison)

Chis Pibble Treats are described on the wiki's Food and Drinks page as a poison version of regular Pibble Treats. They are priced at 20 Pibuckies (≈ $40). Their existence implies an antagonistic supply chain — likely the Chis themselves — and gives the verse light RPG-style consumable mechanics where the same kind of item can buff or harm depending on its source.[13]

Pibberries

Pibberries are a fruit or berry of the Pibble Verse identified as a favorite food of geebles. Their inclusion alongside Pibble Treats suggests there is a broader ecosystem of Pibble Verse flora and snacks not yet fully documented on the wiki.[10][13]

Pibuckies (currency)

Pibuckies are the fictional currency of the Pibble Verse. The single surfaced price point — 20 Pibuckies for a Chis Pibble Treat (annotated as ≈ $40) — implies a conversion rate of roughly two USD per Pibucky, though no exchange rate is officially stated and contributors generally treat the number as flavor rather than economics. The origin of Pibuckies is one of the verse's open questions.[13][5]

Geeble laser guns

While not strictly a food item, the wiki notes that geebles are armed with laser guns, which they use to blast hostile entities such as Chingles and Chis. Combined with the geebles' acidic blood and very hard, resistant skin, this makes them surprisingly well-equipped despite their otherwise gentle treat-asking behavior.[10][13]
