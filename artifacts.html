Sacraments & Provisions

The treats, fruits, currencies, and armaments that sustain daily life in the Pibble Verse — from the humble Pibble Treat to the Geeble's laser-armed devotion.

A Modest Economy

The Sacraments of the Verse

The Pibbleology maintains a small but consequential material culture. A fictional currency — the Pibucky — circulates among the faithful. A base consumable — the Pibble Treat — is craved by Pibbles and Geebles alike. And at least one adversarial counterpart, the Chis Pibble Treat, reminds us that not every offering is wholesome.

What follows is the catalog of authorized sacraments and provisions, as currently affirmed by the Lorewriters and the broader fellowship.

Authorized Catalog

The Core Sacraments

Authorized · Wholesome

Pibble Treats

The base consumable of the Verse. Coveted by Pibbles and Geebles alike. Geebles famously hover around skyscrapers asking humans for Pibble Treats — and fly away politely when refused.

Cost: Freely Given · Status: Foundational

⚠ Counterfeit · Poisonous

Chis Pibble Treats

A poisoned counterfeit of the genuine Pibble Treat. Manufactured or distributed by the Chis. Practitioners are urged to verify packaging carefully; when in doubt, decline.

Cost: 20 Pibuckies (≈ $40) · Status: Avoid

Authorized · Cross-Species

Pibberries

A favored fruit of our Geeble cousins. Pibberries hint at a broader botanical ecology still awaiting fuller canonical revelation. Cultivated through the Pibberry Sustainability Initiative.

Cost: Not Yet Disclosed · Status: Beloved by Geebles
P¥

Authorized · Currency

Pibuckies

The fictional currency of the Pibble Verse. The single surfaced conversion — 20 Pibuckies for one Chis Pibble Treat at ≈ $40 — implies roughly two USD per Pibucky. The true origin of the Pibucky remains an open canonical question.

Conversion: ~$2 USD per Pibucky · Status: Active

Authorized · Defensive

Geeble Laser Guns

Not technically a foodstuff, but a recognized provision. Geebles are armed with laser guns to blast hostile entities such as Chingles and Chis. Paired with acidic blood and unusually hard skin.

Wielders: Geebles · Targets: Adversarial Species

Sacred · Foundational

The Washable Belly

Not an item, strictly — but functionally the most-handled object of devotion in the Verse. Central mechanic of Pibble Petz and to the entire ethical practice of the faith.

Cost: A Gentle Hand · Status: Eternal

A Word of Caution

On the Matter of Counterfeit Treats

The Lorewriters have affirmed it, and the Chis themselves have confirmed it through their actions: not every treat that looks like a Pibble Treat is a Pibble Treat.

The Chis Pibble Treat — a poisoned counterfeit priced at 20 Pibuckies — represents the only known adversarial commodity in the Verse. Our Treat Distribution Initiative actively combats their spread, ensuring that no Pibble unknowingly consumes a Chis-branded variant.

If you encounter a treat of uncertain origin, decline politely. The Geebles will handle the rest.
